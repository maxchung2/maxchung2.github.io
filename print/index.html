Science Fair Brochure

The Science Fair Foundation of BC (SFFBC) is the largest volunteer science outreach network in the province that supports students, educators and Science Fair activity across British Columbia and the Yukon. The organization aims to promote science and technology education by inspiring curiosity through the Science Fair program.

Science Fair Foundation BC came to the agency looking for a design direction for their yearly Team BC results brochure. I helped design a few key pages to establish the overall style and made sure to follow their website's color pallete, fonts, and visual style.

Strategic Growth Partners Brand Guidelines

The Strategic Growth Partners (SGP) is a network of 21 Emerson-certified partners that banded together to deliver Emerson’s products, services and solutions throughout North America.

The agency I was at designed and developed the website template for SGP's 21 partners. To help keep the website and print media styling consistent and on brand for all the websites, I helped to create a brand guideline document outlining the basic design elements, colours, and typography. Examples are also included to show how elements are laid out.
