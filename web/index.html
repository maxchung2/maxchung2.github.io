CPA BC Website Redesign

The Chartered Professional Accountants of British Columbia (CPABC) is a regulatory body that represents over 37,000 CPA members and 5,000 CPA students. The organization is responsible for the following:

  • Training and certification of CPA students
  • Regulation and professional development of its members
  • Protection of the public through its ethical standards and discipline processes

CPABC is looking for a website design refresh to replace their six-year-old website. Following the design direction established by the design lead, we created some mockups for the CPA Education Program. The most challenging part was adapting the content supplied by the client to fit the available widgets.

Equifaira

Equifaira is a team of business professionals that opens the door to private investing for everyone. They help people become a shareholder in a private company that invests in local Canadian private companies.

The client is looking for a new website for more people to learn about what they do and to attract new leads. Following the design direction established by the design lead, we created some mockups for Equifaira's new website. The site is designed in Sketch and built with Wordpress' Elementor plugin.

As a brand element and for visual interest, we included origami objects throughout the pages. We removed the background so it can be placed on any colour background.

FoodPak Coming Soon

FoodPak is a family run business established in 1968 that provides custom-tailored packaging solutions for clients across the Pacific Northwest and beyond.

To drum up excitement for FoodPak's new branding and website refresh, I was tasked with designing a responsive coming soon page. I used the circular elements to draw attention to certain parts of the page, such as the bag in version 1 and the text in version 2. To reduce development time, I reused the same CSS text styles and grid layout.
